Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At SessionZen, we believe that privacy is a fundamental right. Our Chrome extension is designed with a privacy-first approach to help you manage browser tabs and improve productivity without compromising your personal information.

Our Core Promise: All your data remains exclusively on your device. We never transmit data to external servers, cloud services, or third parties. Your browsing history and habits are yours alone.

Data Collection & Usage

SessionZen operates entirely locally on your device. The following explains what data we access and why:

Browser Tab Information

  • URLs & Page Titles: To enable session saving, tab grouping, and restoration
  • Tab Relationships: To understand how tabs are related for intelligent organization
  • Timestamps: To provide productivity insights and usage analytics

User Configuration

  • Focus Mode Settings: Your blocked sites and schedule preferences
  • Saved Sessions: Your named session configurations and tab groups
  • Extension Preferences: Your customization of how SessionZen behaves

Strictly Local Processing

All data processing occurs locally within your browser. No information leaves your device at any time. Our architecture ensures that even if you're offline, all features continue to work perfectly.

What We Never Access

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial or payment details
  • Authentication credentials
  • Personal communications
  • Form data or page content

How We Use Your Data

  • Session Management: Save, organize, and restore your browsing contexts with precision
  • Focus Enhancement: Identify distracting patterns and help maintain productive workflows
  • Performance Optimization: Intelligently manage memory usage by suspending inactive tabs
  • Productivity Analytics: Provide insights into your browsing habits to help you work smarter
  • Tab Organization: Automatically group related tabs and eliminate duplicates

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data resides exclusively in your browser's secured storage
  • Chrome's Security Sandbox: Your data is protected by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
  • No External Transmission: Zero data is sent to servers or external services
  • User-Controlled Retention: You decide how long data is kept and when it's deleted
  • Export Capability: You can export your data for backup or transfer at any time

Your Rights & Controls

We empower you with complete control over your data:

  • Full Visibility: Access all stored data through the extension's settings panel
  • Instant Deletion: Remove all or selected data with a single click
  • Export Freedom: Download your data in standardized JSON format for portability
  • Selective Disabling: Choose which features to enable based on your comfort level
  • Transparent Operations: Every data access is clearly indicated within the interface

Children's Privacy

SessionZen does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Our extension is designed for general audiences and we encourage parents to supervise their children's online activities. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect improvements to our extension or regulatory changes. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Significant changes will be highlighted in the extension's update notes. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed.

Privacy Commitment

SessionZen is built on the principle that your data belongs to you. We've designed our extension to provide powerful productivity features while ensuring that your browsing information never leaves your device. You maintain complete sovereignty over your data at all times.
